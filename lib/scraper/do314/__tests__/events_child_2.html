8th Annual Beer Christmas

Wed Dec 19
6:30PM

​HandleBar
Edit
31 Votes 31 Votes
*It's the most wonderful time FOR A BEER!*

If you're new to our favorite event of the whole year, here's how it works: Drink beer, get gifts, and hang out with Santa! Plus this year we'll have Celia's Yuletide Express at 7pm and a Christmas Drag show at 8pm going for your pleasure and entertainment!

4 Hands will brew up a special Christmas cask just for tonight. More details coming soon.

As always, each pint you drink of our exclusive 4 Hands beer will earn you a surprise gift from under our tree, like a t-shirt, neon sign, can of cat food, or a rare bottle from our cellar.

We wrap more gifts every year, and they run out faster each party. Get here early and walkout happy!

*************************************************************
Twas a week before Christmas, and all through this town
the people were thirsty and had troubles to drown!
But a cask was all ready and about to be tapped,
And oodles of prizes had been carefully wrapped.

The guys down at 4 Hands had filled up our pints
with their famous brew all spiced up nice.
Drinkable cookies were poured then around
A sudsy baked-good delight everyone downed.

When thirsty folks all sought for more than libation,
We found other breweries to join the celebration.
Bottles and caps with shirts and excitation,
Much brewery schwag was put on location!

Each pint got a ticket, and each ticket a chance,
There was more to these prizes than thought at first glance.
And after the cask blew, each guest found with glee,
The taps now all flowed at less of a fee!

The casks were all empty, last call meant no more,
We tipped up our glasses and walked toward the door.
From the bar called a fellow we knew on first sight,
"Hoppy Beer-mas to all, and to all a good night!"
*************************************************************
